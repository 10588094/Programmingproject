Disorder Prevalence

There is an increase in diagnoses of mental disorders such as depression, autism, ADHD and more. Often treatments are not sufficient due to a lack of understanding what these disorders really are. To prevent/treat a disorder it is essential to understand the disorder. An overview of disorder prevalence over the world can help in getting a better understanding and finding possible causes for the increase in diagnoses.

DALY score

The prevalence of disorders is measured with a DALY score. One DALY can be thought of as one lost year of "healthy" life. The sum of these DALYs across the population, or the burden of disease, can be thought of as a measurement of the gap between current health status and an ideal health situation where the entire population lives to an advanced age, free of disease and disability.
